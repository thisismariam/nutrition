Nutritional Database Website User Guide

Welcome to the Nutritional Database Website User Guide! This guide will walk you through the features and functionalities of our website, designed to provide you with easy access to nutritional information for various food items. Whether you're a health-conscious individual or a professional in the nutrition field, this user-friendly platform will help you make informed dietary choices.

Table of Contents:

  1. Accessing the Website
  2. Searching for Food Items
  3. Viewing Nutritional Information
  4. Contact and Support

1. Accessing the Website:

To get started, open your web browser and enter the website's URL: https://nutrition-app-pzng.onrender.com. Press "Enter" to navigate to the website's home page.

2. Searching for Food Items:

On the home page, you will find a simple search bar. Follow these steps to search for a specific food item:

  1. Type the name of the food item you want to search for in the input field.
  2. Click the "Search" button or press "Enter" on your keyboard.

3. Viewing Nutritional Information:

Once you've performed a search, you'll be directed to the results page. Here, you will find detailed nutritional information for the selected food item. The results are presented in a table format, with each column representing a specific nutritional value.

To view nutritional information:

  1. Locate the table on the results page.
  2. Review the columns to find details such as calories, total fat, protein, carbohydrates, and more.
  3. Scroll through the table to explore additional nutritional values.
  4. If you are having trouble reading your food item's resulting nutritional data due to too many results listed, copy the exact Item Name and paste it into the search bar to retrieve this result only, and you will be able to easily read its specific nutritional information.

4. Contact and Support:

If you encounter any issues while using the website or have questions about the displayed nutritional information, please feel free to contact our support team. You can reach us by emailing us with your name, email address, subject, and message.

Additional Tips:

  • If the search results do not display the food item you're looking for, try entering different variations of the food name or broader keywords.
  • You can bookmark the website for easy access in the future.
